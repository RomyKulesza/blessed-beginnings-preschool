# Editing the Blessed Beginnings Website — Plain-English Guide

You do **not** need to know any code to run this website. Everything is edited through a simple online dashboard. This guide walks you through the common things you'll want to do.

---

## 1. Logging in

1. In your web browser, go to your website address followed by **`/admin`**
   (for example: `www.blessedbeginningspreschool.org/admin`).
2. Click **Login with Netlify Identity** and enter your email and password.
   *(You'll receive an email invitation the first time — click the link in it to set your password.)*

You'll land on the dashboard. On the left you'll see two sections:
- **Website Pages** — the main pages that already exist (Home, Our Mission, etc.)
- **Add / Edit Pages** — where you create brand-new pages.

---

## 2. Changing words on an existing page

1. Click **Website Pages**.
2. Click the page you want to change (e.g. **Home Page**).
3. Click into any box and type your new text.
4. Click **Publish → Publish now** (top right).
5. Wait about a minute, then refresh your website — your change is live. ✅

> 💡 To start a new paragraph, leave a **blank line** between paragraphs.

---

## 3. Replacing a photo

1. Open the page (as above).
2. Find the photo field and click on the current image.
3. Click **Upload** and choose a new picture from your computer (or pick one you've used before).
4. Click **Publish → Publish now**.

You can also manage **all** photos in one place: click **Media** at the top of the dashboard to upload, view, or delete any image on the site.

> 💡 For staff headshots, square photos look best. For wide banner photos, landscape works best.

---

## 4. Adding a brand-new page

1. Click **Add / Edit Pages** on the left, then **New Page** (top right).
2. Fill in:
   - **Page title** (e.g. "Summer Camp")
   - **Header color** — pick a rainbow color for the top banner.
   - **Show in top menu?** — leave **on** to add it to the website's menu automatically.
3. Under **Sections**, click **Add Section** and choose what kind of section you want:

   | Section | What it does |
   |---|---|
   | **Heading** | A big title |
   | **Text** | A paragraph (or several) |
   | **Image** | A single photo |
   | **Image + Text** | A photo beside some text |
   | **Photo Gallery** | A grid of photos |
   | **Info Cards** | 2–3 little boxes with a title and text |
   | **Video** | A video (paste a YouTube or Google Drive link) |
   | **Buttons** | Clickable buttons |
   | **Color Banner** | A colorful "call to action" strip with a button |

4. Add as many sections as you like. **Drag them by the handle to reorder**, or delete ones you don't want.
5. Click **Publish → Publish now**. Your new page is live and in the menu. 🎉

---

## 5. Changing the menu, phone number, or address

1. Click **Website Pages → Site Settings**.
2. Here you can change the preschool name, phone, address, the accreditation line, the logo, and the **Navigation menu** (the links across the top).
3. To reorder menu links, drag them. To add one, click **Add**. To remove one, click the trash icon.
4. **Publish → Publish now**.

---

## 6. Adding a video (e.g. a virtual tour)

- **Google Drive:** open the video in Drive → Share → set to *"Anyone with the link."* Then use this address, replacing the ID with your file's ID:
  `https://drive.google.com/file/d/YOUR-FILE-ID/preview`
- **YouTube:** use the "embed" link, which looks like `https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO-ID`.

Paste that link into any **Video** section (or the Home Page's "Virtual tour video" field).

---

## 7. Golden rules

- **Always click "Publish → Publish now"** — nothing goes live until you do.
- Changes appear on the real website about **a minute** after publishing.
- If something looks wrong, you can always edit it again — you can't break anything permanently.
- Every image can be swapped, every word can be changed, and you can add or remove pages anytime.

---

## 8. Changing the colors or fonts

Want a different look? You can change that too — no developer needed.

1. Click **Website Pages → Design — Colors & Fonts**.
2. Change the **Main color** (buttons and links), the **text** or **background** color, any of the six **rainbow** colors, or pick a new **heading/body font**.
3. Click **Publish → Publish now**. The new look applies across the entire website.

> 💡 The colors are set to match your rainbow logo. If you experiment and want to get back, the original values are: Main color `#3aa935`, Text `#262626`, Background `#fbf8f2`, and rainbow Red `#e30613`, Orange `#ec6608`, Yellow `#ffc700`, Green `#3aa935`, Blue `#0072bc`, Purple `#8f2d8f`.

## 9. The loading screen & the "page not found" screen

Even these are yours to edit — no code.

- **Website Pages → Loading & Error Screens.**
  - **Loading screen message** — optional words under the logo while a page opens (leave blank for just the logo + dots).
  - **"Page not found" heading, message, and button** — the friendly screen someone sees if they follow a broken or old link.
- The **logo** on both screens comes from **Site Settings → Logo**, so updating your logo updates them automatically.

Click **Publish → Publish now** when done.

---

*Everything on this website — words, photos, pages, menu, colors, fonts, and even the loading and error screens — is yours to change right here. Nothing requires a developer.*
