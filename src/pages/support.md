      ## GRACE Scholarship Fund
      No child should be denied the opportunity to attend our preschool due to a family's financial hardship. The **GRACE Scholarship Fund** — *Giving Real opportunities for All Children's Education* — offers reduced tuition to families in need. Every gift, of any size, helps a child attend Blessed Beginnings. To donate or to request a financial-assistance application, please [contact us](/contact/).
  - type: text

      ## Our Wish List
      Small donations of everyday supplies go a long way in our classroom. We are always grateful for:

      - 5 oz. paper cups
      - Napkins
      - Facial tissue
      - Elmer's or Target-brand glue sticks
      - Clorox or store-brand disinfecting wipes
      - Monetary donations toward our GRACE Scholarship Program

      Thank you for your generosity!
